About me

Hello, my name is Dima Vakerinets. I am from the city of Kharkov, Ukraine. I am 32 years old.I taught and graduated from school, then I went to study and graduated from the Kharkov Polytechnic Institute with a degree in cars and automotive business. After university, I worked at the New Post Office, at the moment I work as a hemlock for furniture products. In the fall of 2022, I went to courses at the Lemon School and began to study the profession of website development. During the training, I studied HTML5, CSS and got acquainted with the Jawa Script programming languages. From personal qualities I can note: diligence, punctuality, teamwork for a common result and not being afraid to learn something new.
